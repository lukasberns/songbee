Adding and editing songs

To add a new song, click on the "add song" toolbutton at the top of the main playlist window. You can edit an existing song by right-clicking[1] on the song name while editing a playlist:

Figure 4.1. Editing an existing song

Once you have selected a song to edit, or chosen "Add Song", you will be taken to the song editor window. The song editor window looks like this:

Figure 4.2. Editing an existing song

This consists of two parts; the right hand part is an automatically-generated preview. The left part is where you enter all the details of the current song: its title, key, year, and so on. The box at the bottom is where you enter the lyrics. The lyrics are entered using a couple of conventions:

  • Verses, choruses and bridges are entered as paragraphs, with a blank line separating them. Lines breaks are preserved inside a verse. To begin a chorus or bridge, the first line should be "Chorus:" or "Bridge:" respectively, with the text of the chorus/bridge immediately following and with no blank line in between.

  • When entering Japanese furigana, do so like this: (漢|かん). That is to say, place the expression in brackets; put the main text first, then a pipe sign (|), and then the furigana. The furigana will be displayed above the main text. You should be able to see this on the preview.


[1] (Mac: Ctrl-clicking.
